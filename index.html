Market Functions

Aggregated Demand Function

$$Q_d = 400 - 15P$$

Slope (-15) indicates high price sensitivity.

Aggregated Supply Function

$$Q_s = 20 + 5P$$

Base supply 20, increases with price.

Equilibrium Calculation ($$Q_d = Q_s$$)

$$400 - 15P = 20 + 5P$$

$$P^* = 19$$ | $$Q^* = 115$$
